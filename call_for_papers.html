Call for Papers

……………………………………………………………………………………………

Authors are invited to submit original unpublished work that is not currently under consideration for publication
elsewhere via the various tracks hosted by INCOME 2020.

  • Track I: Fluid Mechanics and Energy
  • Track II: Material Engineering
  • Track III: Structural Mechanics
  • Track IV: Manufacturing and Design
  • Track V: Other research work related to mechanical engineering

Track I: Fluid Mechanics and Energy
  • Combustion and biofuels
  • Heat and Mass Transfer
  • Fluid-structure interaction and Flow Control
  • Aerodynamic and Aero Acoustics
  • Turbulence and Instabilities
  • Rheology and Complex Fluids
  • Advances in Aerospace Technology and Autonomous Systems
  • Turbomachinery and Thermodynamics

 

Track II: Material Engineering
  • Composites, Smart, Nano and Micro Materials
  • Tooling Testing and Evaluation of Materials
  • Surface Engineering, Coatings and Tribology
  • Materials Behavior, Forming and Processing
  • Powder metallurgy and ceramic forming
  • Corrosion
  • Plasticity, Fatigue, Fracture Mechanics and Stress Analysis

 

Track III: Structural Mechanics

  • Vibrations, Acoustics and Noise Control
  • Diagnostics and Maintenance
  • Safety Engineering and Risk Analysis
  • NDE, Structural Health Monitoring and Prognosis
  • Robotics, Mechatronics and Mobile Platforms
  • Automotive Engineering, Transportation Systems and Multibody Dynamics
  • Instrumentation and Control
  • Production planning, scheduling and Technology

 

Track IV: Manufacturing and Design processes
  • Manufacturing Processes, Technology and Simulation
  • Metrology and Quality control
  • CNC, CAD, CAM, FMS, CIM, Industrial Design and reverse engineering
  • Bioengineering and Bio-Mechanics
  • Advanced Metal Forming, Welding & Casting Techniques
  • Manufacturing Process and Ergonomics
  • Virtual Design, Prototyping and Visualization
    • Optimal and Multidisciplinary Design

 

Track V: Other Research related to Mechanical Engineering
  • Artificial intelligence applied, optimization methods
  • Automation and control
  • Design and manufacture, medical device manufacturing
  • Heat treatments, micro-structure and materials properties
  • Joining and fracture mechanics
  • Lubricants and lubrication
  • Machinability and formability of materials
  • Laser Material Interactions
  • Operations Research
  • Project Management and Supply Chain Management
  • Quality Control and Management
  • Reliability and Maintenance Engineering
  • Service Innovation and Management
  • Decision Making
  • Systems Modeling and Simulation
  • Technology and Knowledge Management
  • Rapid manufacturing technologies and prototyping, remanufacturing
  • Recycling, materials and industrial wastes, products and recycling systems
  • Renewable energies technology
  • Sustainable and green manufacturing
  • Tribology and surface engineering
  • Ergonomics
  • Nuclear Engineering
  • Mathematical Modelling
  • Nanotechnology
  • Sensors and Transducers
  • Uncertainty of measurement
